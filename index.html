franciscopereira.art
francisco pereira is a visual artist born in brasília, brazil. he has lived and worked in barcelona since 2019, where he completed a master's in art history at the university of barcelona in 2021. since 2022, he has taken part in collective and solo exhibitions.
elemental geometric forms derived from architecture and traditional tiles — particularly the triangular mosaics shared between barcelona and brasília — serve as matrices. as they repeat and fragment, the publication space and architectural surfaces become sites of investigation.

education

  • 2018 bachelor's in fine arts, university of brasília
